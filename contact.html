Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple projects like business cards take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive branding or website projects can take 4-8 weeks.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! We include multiple revision rounds in all our packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What file formats do you provide?

We provide all necessary file formats including high-resolution print files (PDF, AI, EPS) and web-optimized formats (PNG, JPG, SVG).

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and have streamlined processes for remote collaboration and communication.
